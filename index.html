About this service

What is this?

LTS is a focused typing space created by a UX designer and book lover. I carefully tuned the typewriter sounds and the rhythm of the keys to build an immersive environment for readers.

Why type literature?

Typing a story word for word is a form of deep reading. It is not about speed. It is about slowing down to experience the rhythm and craftsmanship in every sentence.

What we collect

When you sign in, we receive your name and email from the OAuth provider. We store typing session data: WPM, accuracy, duration, and which passage you typed.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the service. We do not sell your data.

Data deletion

You may delete your account at any time. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
