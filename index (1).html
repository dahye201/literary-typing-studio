Literary Typing Studio
About this service

What is this?

Literary Typing Studio is a typing practice platform built around the opening passages of the world's greatest novels.

Why type literature?

Typing a great sentence, letter by letter, is a form of close reading. You feel the rhythm and begin to understand why the sentence is the way it is.

Copyright

All passages are from works published before 1928 and are in the public domain in the United States.

Privacy Policy
Last updated January 2025

What we collect

When you sign in, we receive your name and email from the OAuth provider. We store typing session data: WPM, accuracy, duration, and which passage you typed.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to provide the service. We do not sell your data.

Data deletion

You may delete your account at any time. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
